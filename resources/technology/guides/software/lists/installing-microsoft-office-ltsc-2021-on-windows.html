Installing Microsoft Office LTSC 2021 on Windows

Preparatory Tasks

Disabling Real-Time Protection
  1. Start Menu > Windows Security > Virus & threat protection > "Manage settings" under "Virus & threat protection settings"
  2. Turn off "Real-time protection"
  3. Exit
Collecting Tools
  1. On the desktop of the target system, download and extract the following archives
    • Office C2R Install | Office 2021 Installer
    • KMS_VL_ALL_AIO | KMS & Volume License Activator

Installation

  1. Run "OInstall.exe"
  2. Click the "Microsoft Office 2016" dropdown and click "Microsoft Office 2019-2021"
  3. Click "x64"
  4. Uncheck all checked applications under "Single Products:"
  5. Check/uncheck the applications you'd like installed under the "ProPlus" dropdown
  6. Click "Install Office"
  7. Wait for the installation to complete, then click "Close"
  8. On the "OInstall.exe" window, click the "Utilities" tab, then click "Office RETAIL => VL"
  9. Exit

Activation

  1. Right-click "KMS_VL_ALL_AIO.cmd" then click "Run as administrator"
  2. Type 2 to "Install Activation Auto-Renewal"
  3. Wait for the process to complete then exit

Configuration

  1. Open any Office application > File > click "Options"
  2. General > uncheck "Show the Start screen when this application starts"
  3. Proofing > check "Check grammar with spelling" and "Show readability statistics"
  4. Click "Grammar" and change to "Grammar & Refinements"
  5. Customise Ribbon > Customise the Ribbon: > uncheck "Add-ins" and "Help"
  6. Quick Access Toolbar > Customise Quick Access Toolbar: > click "AutoSave" then click "<< Remove"
  7. Click "OK"
  8. Right-click the status bar and uncheck all except "Zoom", "Zoom Slider", "Page Number", "View Indicator"/"Cell Mode" and "Word Count"
