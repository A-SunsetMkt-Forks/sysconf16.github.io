  2. Right-click the following links, "Save link as", and save inside your downloads folder
    • privacy-script.sh

System Preferences

  1. Click the Apple Logo on the Menu Bar > click "System Preferences..."
Apple ID

Desktop & Screen Saver

Dock & Menu Bar

  3. Dock & Menu Bar > change "Minimise windows using:" to "Scale effect"
  4. Dock & Menu Bar > uncheck "Double-click a window's title bar to"
  5. Dock & Menu Bar > check "Minimise windows into application icon"
  6. Dock & Menu Bar > uncheck "Animate opening applications"
  7. Dock & Menu Bar > check "Show indicators for open applications"
  8. Dock & Menu Bar > uncheck "Show recent applications in Dock"
  9. Dock & Menu Bar > uncheck "Automatically hide and show the menu bar in full screen"
  10. Bluetooth > uncheck "Show in Menu Bar"
  11. AirDrop > uncheck "Show in Menu Bar"

Software Update

Network

Bluetooth

Sound

Finder

  1. Click "Finder" on the Menu Bar > click "Preferences"
Preferences
  1. General > uncheck all except "External disks"
  2. General > change "New Finder windows show:" and click your username
  3. General > uncheck "Open folders in tabs instead of new windows"
  4. Tags > select each tag then click "-"
  5. Sidebar > only check "Applications", "Desktop", "Documents", "Downloads", "Pictures", "Movies", "Music", "Hard disks", "External disks", "CDs, DVDs and iOS Devices"
  6. Advanced > uncheck "Show warning before changing and extension", "Show warning before removing from iCloud Drive" and "Remove items from the Bin after 30 days"
  7. Advanced > change "When performing a search" to "Search the Current Folder"
  8. Exit
View
  1. Click "as List"
  2. Click "Show Path Bar"
Toolbar
  1. Right-click the white space on the toolbar, then click "Customise Toolbar..."
  2. Remove all except "Back/Forward", "View" and "Search"

  11. Security > uncheck "Warn when visiting a fraudulent website"

  14. Websites > Reader > change "When visiting other websites" to "Off"
  15. Websites > Content Blockers > change "When visiting other websites" to "Off"

Toolbar

Managing Telemetry, Data Collection & Services

privacy.sexy Script
  1. Finder > Applications > Utilities > click "Terminal"
  2. Copy and paste the following commands one by one
    • cd ~/Downloads
    • chmod +x privacy-script.sh
    • ./privacy-script.sh
  3. Enter your password and hit enter on your keyboard
  4. Exit
TinkerTool
  1. Run "TinkerTool.dmg" from your downloads and install the application
  2. Finder > Applications > click "TinkerTool", click "Open" then click "Understood"
  3. File > click "Import..." > navigate to your downloads, select "SYSCONF16.ttps", click "Import", click "Yes" then "OK"
  4. Exit and restart

Installing & Configuring Basic Applications

AppCleaner
  1. Extract "AppCleaner_X.X.X.zip" from your downloads and move "AppCleaner.app" to your applications
  2. Finder > Applications > click "AppCleaner", click "Open" then click "Understood"
  3. Click the list view icon in the top-right
  4. Search for "TinkerTool" > click "TinkerTool" then click "Remove"
  5. Repeat for any other applications you no longer require
  6. Exit
Keka
  1. Run "Keka-X.X.XX.dmg" from your downloads and install the application
  2. Finder > Applications > click "Keka", click "Open" then click "Understood"
  3. Click "Keka" on the Menu Bar > click "Preferences"
  4. General > change "Default action:" to "Perform automatic action"
  5. General > uncheck "Enable Notification Centre integration"
  6. General > check "Close Keka when no windows are open"
  7. General > uncheck "Automatically check for updates"
  8. Appearance > uncheck "Use custom tasks counter in the Dock"
  9. Compression > change "Default format" to "7Z"
  10. Compression > change "Default method" to "Slowest, more compression"
  11. Compression > change "Save to location" to "Next to original file"
  12. Compression > change "Name of new files" to "Same as original file"
  13. Compression > check "Use parent folder name when compressing multiple files"
  14. Compression > change "Play a sound after a compression" to "None"
  15. Compression > check "Exclude Mac resource forks"
  16. Compression > uncheck "Show file in Finder after compression"
  17. Extraction > change "Extract to location" to "Next to original file"
  18. Extraction > change "Play a sound after an extraction" to "None"
  19. Extraction > check "Exclude Mac resource forks"
  20. Extraction > uncheck "Show content in Finder after extraction"
  21. Extraction > check "Remove bad password header structure extractions"
  22. Extraction > check "Rename the recognised extension from the subfolder"
  23. File Access > click "+", select "Macintosh HD" then click "Give access to Keka"
  24. Performance > change "Maximum simultaneous operations" to "1"
  25. Performance > change "Maximum simultaneous compressions" to "1"
  26. Performance > change "Maximum simultaneous extractions" to "1"
  27. Finder Extension > only check "Show Keka actions in Finder's contextual menu" and "Use "Open with Keka" as the primary item action"
  28. Exit
AdGuard for Safari
  1. Click the Apple Logo on the Menu Bar > click "App Store..."
  2. Search for and install AdGuard for Safari
  3. Open Safari and click "Safari" on the Menu Bar > click "Preferences"
  4. Extensions > check "AdGuard Advanced Blocking" and "AdGuard General" then exit
  5. Finder > Applications > click "AdGuard for Safari"
  6. General > click "Import settings" > navigate to your downloads, select "adguard-settings-2022414_9354.json" then click "Open"
  7. Exit
