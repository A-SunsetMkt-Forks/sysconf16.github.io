Sideloading on iOS & iPadOS

Preparatory Tasks

Enabling Developer Mode
  1. On your iOS or iPadOS device, open Settings
  2. Privacy & Security > Developer Mode
  3. Toggle the switch "On" and follow the instructions to restart
Collecting Tools
  1. Download and install Sideloadly to a PC using the following link
    • Sideloadly | .ipa Installer
  2. If you're using Windows, uninstall any installations of iTunes present on the target system, then download and install iTunes using the following link
    • iTunes | Win32 iTunes Installer
  3. Download the .ipa files you'd like to install to your iOS or iPadOS device from the links below and save them to the downloads folder on your PC
    • Spotilife | iOS Spotify Client with Premium Features
    • uYou+ | iOS YouTube Client with Adblocking
    • Appdb | iOS App Aggregator (to search for other apps not listed above)
  4. Uninstall any installations of an app already present on the target device before attempting to install a sideloaded copy
Creating a Temporary Apple ID (optional)
  1. On your iOS or iPadOS device, open Settings
  2. Mail > Accounts > tap "Add Account", then tap "iCloud"
  3. Tap "Create a new Apple ID"
  4. Fill in anonymous information for "First name", "Last name" and "Date of birth"
  5. Tap "Next" and turn off "Apple News & Announcements"
  6. Enter an email address, tap "Next" then type a password into the "Password" and "Verify" fields
  7. Tap "Next", then change the country code to "+92 (Pakistan)"
  8. Enter "317 6765544" as the phone number, then tap "Next"
  9. Tap "Didn't get a verification code?", then "Verify Later" and "Continue"
  10. Tap "Agree"

Sideloading an App

Installation
  1. Connect your iOS or iPadOS device to your PC and launch Sideloadly
  2. Click "IPA" and navigate to a .ipa file saved in your downloads folder
  3. Type your Apple ID email address into the "Apple account:" field
  4. Click "Start"
  5. Enter your Apple ID password into the field when prompted
  6. Enter the verification code that appears on your iOS or iPadOS device into the field when prompted
  7. Disconnect the device once complete and exit
Trusting the Developer
  1. On your iOS or iPadOS device, open Settings
  2. General > VPN & Device Management
  3. Tap your Apple ID, then tap "Trust [Apple ID]"
  4. Tap "Trust" then exit

Info

  • Sideloaded apps will no longer open after 7 days as they will need re-signing
  • This is due to the limitations of a free-tier Apple Developer ID
  • Repeat steps 1, 2 and 4 of the installation process every 7 days to bypass this limitation
